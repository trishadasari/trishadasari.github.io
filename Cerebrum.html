About the Cerebellum

The Cerebellum is located at the back of the brain close to the brainstem. It is responsible for balance, walking and multi-step motor movements. According to, “https://www.cancer.gov/publications/dictionaries/cancer-terms/def/cerebellum” , it states, “The portion of the brain in the back of the head between the cerebrum and the brain stem. The cerebellum controls balance for walking and standing, and other complex motor functions.” From my inferences I can tell that if the cerebellum is affected, it would cause the person to stumble around. But, other than that, electronics apparently do no harm to the cerebellum whatsoever.
