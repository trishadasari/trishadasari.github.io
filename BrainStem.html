About the Brain Stem

The brainstem is located at the bottom of the brain and is connected to your spinal cord at the top. The brainstem is one of the most vital needs for a human to survive. It is broken down into 3 parts starting from the top, the midbrain, pons, and medulla oblongata. According to, “https://www.physio-pedia.com/Brainstem” , “It is responsible for many vital functions of life, such as breathing, consciousness, blood pressure, heart rate, and sleep.” The brain stem also carries information from your nerves up to the brain through the spinal cord.
