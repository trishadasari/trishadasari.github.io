About the Temporal Lobe

The Temporal Lobe sits right behind your ears and is dedicated to processing information. Think about it Temporal, and temporary kinda similar right? According to “Brain Map: Temporal Lobes | Queensland Health” ,” The temporal lobes are also believed to play an important role in processing affect/emotions, language, and certain aspects of visual perception.” Further on, I read that damage to the Temporal Lobe can be things such as inability to remember faces, long-term memory, and difficulty understanding spoken words.
